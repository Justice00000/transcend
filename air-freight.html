      Frequently Asked Question

      Answers to frequently asked questions.

      • What types of goods can be shipped via air freight?
        Our air freight services can handle a wide range of goods, including general cargo, perishable items, hazardous materials, and oversized shipments. However, certain restrictions may apply based on airline regulations and destination country policies. Please contact us for specific details regarding your shipment.
      • How do I determine the cost of air freight?
        The cost of air freight is determined by several factors, including the weight and dimensions of the shipment, the type of goods, the shipping route, and the required delivery speed. For an accurate quote, please provide us with the detailed information about your shipment.
      • What documentation is required for air freight shipping?
        The necessary documentation for air freight includes a commercial invoice, packing list, air waybill, and any required export/import licenses or certificates. Our team will assist you in preparing and submitting all necessary documents to ensure a smooth shipping process.
      • How can I ensure my goods are properly packaged for air freight?
        Proper packaging is crucial for the safety of your goods during air transport. We recommend using sturdy, high-quality packaging materials and following industry best practices for securing and cushioning your items. Our team can provide guidance and packaging services to ensure your goods are well-protected.
