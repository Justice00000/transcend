      Frequently Asked Question

      Answers to frequently asked questions.

      • What types of goods can be shipped via ocean freight?
        Our ocean freight services can handle a wide range of goods, including general cargo, bulk commodities, refrigerated goods, hazardous materials, and oversized shipments. However, certain restrictions may apply based on destination country regulations. Please contact us for specific details regarding your shipment.
      • How is the cost of ocean freight determined?
        The cost of ocean freight is determined by several factors, including the volume and weight of the cargo, the type of goods, the shipping route, and any additional services required, such as customs clearance or insurance. We provide competitive rates and can offer a detailed quote based on your specific needs.
      • How long does ocean freight shipping take?
        The transit time for ocean freight shipping varies based on the origin and destination ports, as well as the shipping route. Typically, it can take anywhere from a few weeks to over a month. We provide estimated delivery times based on your specific shipment details.
      • What documentation is required for ocean freight shipping?
        Commonly required documents for ocean freight shipping include the Bill of Lading, commercial invoice, packing list, Certificate of Origin, and any necessary customs documentation. Our team will guide you through the documentation process to ensure compliance with all regulations.
